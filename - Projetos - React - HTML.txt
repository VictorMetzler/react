

	HTML
	
		section
		
			The div element has no special meaning. It is often used as a block of children elements. The section element
			introduced in HTML5 standard is used to group related elements, such as a subsection of a long article.
			
		A <footer> element typically contains:

			authorship information
			copyright information
			contact information
			sitemap
			back to top links
			related documents
